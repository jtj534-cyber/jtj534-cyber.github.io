Binary and decimal converter

Computers use binary (0s and 1s) instead of decimal numbers. This tool converts a decimal number into binary.

Try small numbers like 5, 10, or 42.

In binary, each position represents a power of 2. For example, the binary number 1010 means 1×8 + 0×4 + 1×2 + 0×1 = 10 in decimal. Binary is used in computers because hardware works with two states: on and off.
